Located in a lush green setting, high on a hill overlooking Narrabeen lake is Idler's Hill. Our holiday home is in a quiet area on a long meandering street. 7 mins drive to Narrabeen beach & lagoon, supermarkets & B-line bus to the city. North Narrabeen is on the northern beaches so you have access to the best beaches in Sydney such as Palmy, Avalon, Whale beach and Pittwater. The location is approx 40-50 mins drive from central Sydney but feels a world away. The view is rather magical too ;)...
